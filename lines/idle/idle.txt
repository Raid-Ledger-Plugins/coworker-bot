# Ambient mutterings, used when nothing matched.
...is anyone else cold?
I filed that report. Nobody reads the reports.
The fluorescent lights hum at a frequency only I can hear.
Just stretching my legs. Such as they are.
Another productive day at the office.
I left a memo. You should read the memo.
Do you ever feel watched? No? Just me, then.
The vending machine ate my coupon again.
Back to work. Always back to work.
